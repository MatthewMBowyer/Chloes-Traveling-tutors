What is CTT?

CTT helps learners grow through personalised support, confidence-building lessons, and flexible tutoring either in person or online.

01

In-person or online

Choose the option that works best for your family. We can come to you or connect online.

02

Matched to your needs

We get to know each learner and match them with a tutor suited to their goals.

03

Support across grades

From primary to senior levels, we support a wide range of subjects and learning needs.

Every child learns differently

No two children are the same. That is why we adapt our teaching to suit how each learner thinks, understands, remembers, and grows.

By matching our teaching to the way your child learns best, we help them build understanding, confidence, and a genuine love of learning.

👁

Visual learners

Learn best through images, diagrams, colour, examples, and clear visual structure.

👂

Auditory learners

Understand best by listening, discussing, repeating, and explaining aloud.

✋

Practical learners

Thrive with hands-on activities, examples, practice questions, and real-world links.

✓

Step-by-step learners

Benefit from structure, guided practice, simple routines, and repeated reinforcement.

How CTT started

Chloe's Travelling Tutors began from a passion for helping learners feel confident, supported, and capable. What started as a small tutoring service has grown into a trusted team of tutors who love making a positive difference in students' lives.

Today, CTT connects families with caring, qualified tutors across the community and online.
